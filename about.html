About Us

Connecting You with Fast, Reliable Internet

TS11 Ghana Limited, a Ghanaian-owned company, specializes in delivering high-speed fiber broadband internet and comprehensive IT solutions, including professional CCTV camera installations. We are committed to providing reliable, secure, and cost-effective services tailored to the specific needs of residential clients and small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).


Services

Connecting You with Fast, Reliable Internet

TS11 proudly offers Fiber Broadband services, delivering fast, reliable, and value-for-money internet solutions to individuals, residential areas, estates, and SMEs. We provide a variety of data packages tailored to suit different needs and budgets, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the benefits of high-speed internet.

In addition to our broadband services, we offer comprehensive IT solutions, including advanced security services such as CCTV camera installations and surveillance systems. As part of our technical offerings, we also provide professional configuration and support for Cisco and MikroTik routers, ensuring optimal network performance and security. Our commitment is to make both quality internet and reliable technology solutions accessible and affordable for all.
